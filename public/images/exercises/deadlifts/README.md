# Deadlifts (ex_008)

## Exercise Details

**Category**: strength  
**Subcategory**: weights  
**Difficulty**: advanced  
**Equipment**: barbell

### Target Muscles
- **Primary**: back, legs, glutes
- **Secondary**: core, arms

### Exercise Metrics
- **Calories Burned Per Minute**: 8.5
- **Default Duration**: 300 seconds
- **Default Sets**: 4
- **Default Reps**: 8
- **Rest Time**: 120 seconds

### Instructions
1. Stand with feet hip-width apart, bar over mid-foot
2. Bend at hips and knees to grip bar
3. Keep back straight, lift bar by extending hips and knees
4. Lower bar back to ground with control

### Tips
- Keep bar close to body
- Don't round back
- Drive through heels

### Variations
- **Romanian Deadlifts** (Difficulty: same)
- **Sumo Deadlifts** (Difficulty: same)
